Deep Fried Oreos

Learn how to make this delicious desert!

It couldn't be simpler to make deep-fried Oreos at home: Make the batter, dip the cookies in the batter,in batches until they're golden brown.
Dust the finished Oreos with powdered sugar for a perfect presentation.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
  • one cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (18 ounce) package cream-filled chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo)
  • 1 cup pancake mix
